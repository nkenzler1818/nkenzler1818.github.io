About Me

I'm a first-generation college student pursuing a Master of Science in Marketing after completing my undergraduate degree in Business Administration, both at the University of Arizona. I'm drawn to roles where strategy, communication, and execution intersect — especially in fast-paced, high-performing environments.

Across operations, marketing support, and client-facing roles, I've developed a practical understanding of how businesses function day-to-day. I've managed multiple client relationships concurrently, supported team operations under pressure, and contributed to early brand and go-to-market efforts.

This skill has helped me build trust with clients, support better operational decisions, and contribute more effectively in environments where clarity drives outcomes.

Alongside my graduate studies, I'm intentionally building a strong analytics and AI foundation through Tableau and SQL certifications while exploring how AI tools and automation can improve marketing workflows and decision-making.
